the world is a fixed size consisting of tiles
tiles can have blocks and walls
tiles class only holds the most important info, so to hold as little space as possible
additional info can be hooked onto a tile, if needed

rendering tiles gather all the tiles in the area and create an image, that is then rendered
each tile has a tileset, and according to the nearby tiles, the renderer will pick the tileset image it's supposed to render

light is on a separate map, with 1 pixel representing 1 tile with a level of light between 0 and 15 (16 values)
light is rendered once, and cached
